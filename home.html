About

A collaborator
at heart.

I'm a filmmaker living in Austin, TX — drawn equally to shooting, writing, directing, and the creative friction of building something real with people who care about craft.

What I've learned over the years is that I thrive when I'm building something with others — especially when the work is thoughtful, well-crafted, and rooted in story. I bring a wide range of experience and deep curiosity to every project, and care just as much about the process as the product.

Grew up in Weston, Connecticut. Went to Lehigh University. Moved to Tulsa as a Teach For America corps member. Now in Austin with my wife and our dog, Moose.
